How to play

Character control

Moving

Character is moved using mouse. Click on map for moving character

Starting a fight

Ctrl + click on a character to attack him. Should appear a sword instead of cursor to attack someone.

Moving units in fight

Click on a square under a unit to select the unit, then click on another square to move or attack a unit

Unit can be moved on squares highlighted and can attack on square with an X on it

If you don't want to move/attack an unit, press defend or D button

Inventory

Items are moved with drag&drop

To activate an item, drag&drop it to the right place on character

To use a potion mouse over on potion in inventory and press the button `Use`

Units

Units can be reorganized using drag&drop. When you mouse over on an unit slot, it will appear a spinner to choose how many units to move.

Units from first and last slot don't enter in a fight, and are not lost after loosing a fight

Shops

Buying

To buy an item/unit from a shop click on it, mouse over on an item and click buy. The item will automatically appear on inventory/units slots if there is an empty slot.

Selling

Drag&Drop an item on a shop and will be sold

Winning a fight

If you defeat the opponent's hero fight is ended and you are declared as winner.

Training

You can train the following abilities:

  • Damage
  • Defense
  • Leadership
  • Vitality

Damage

In order to train damage hit enter and type /addDamage <number> where <number> can have maximum value `Add points` (that are gained by leveling up)

By training Damage your hero will perform a greater damage on opponents

Defense

Same as for damage, but command is /addDefense <number>

By training Defense opponents will perform a smaller damage on your hero

Leadership

Command: /addLeadership <number>

Will increase hero's leadership by <number>*5

By training this ability you will be able to command more units.

For example if you have 100 leadership and an archer leadership cost is 30, you can command maximum 3 archers.

Vitality

Command: /addVitality <number>

Will increase maximum life of your hero
